About the Pre-RMO Exam

The Pre-Regional Mathematical Olympiad abbreviated as PRMO is a preliminary examination for qualifying into the main Regional Mathematical Olympiad (RMO) examination. In order to appear for the RMO exam, it becomes ‘mandatory’ that students qualify the PRMO exam.

The National Board for Higher Mathematics (NBHM) monitors all the activities of Mathematical Olympiad Activity.

The exam is conducted by Mathematics Teachers’ Association (India) (MTA) which is recognized by the NBHM.


Eligibility for PRMO-2019

I. Candidates must be citizens of India

II. Candidates should be born on or after August 1, 2019

III. Candidates should be studying in class 8, 9, 10, 11 or 12
